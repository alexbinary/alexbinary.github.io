First of all I want to say a big thanks to the people who read this blog every 
week since the beginning. You helped make this blog into what it is today, and I
always appreciate your feedback.

I have been publishing a post every week since the beginning. It has been
very exciting, but also challenging. One week is not a long time to come up with
something interesting to share, do some research, find the right approach, find
illustrations, and write the actual post.

As much as I enjoy writing and sharing, I have other projects, and of course, a
job that eat up a lot of my time. In the recent weeks, it has become clear that
I will not be able to keep up with a weekly schedule.

If I want to keep doing  this blog, I need to find a schedule that I can keep up
with. That's why so starting today, posts will stop coming out every week, and 
start coming up every month instead. I will publish a new post on the second
thursday of each month.

Thanks again to all my readers, and see you on May 9th!
